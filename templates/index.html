Voice Enabled


Chat with your AI just like you would with a friend—ask for financial advice, get personalized recommendations, and make informed money decisions without the stress. Whether you need to track expenses, set realistic budgets, or gain insights into your spending habits, your AI is here to help in a casual, approachable way. No complicated jargon, no overwhelming data—just simple, smart, and friendly financial guidance whenever you need it.


Personal Experience


Enjoy a truly personalized experience with your AI, designed to understand your unique wants and needs. Get tailored advice on your spending habits, receive insights that align with your financial goals, and make smarter money decisions effortlessly. Whether you’re looking to cut back on unnecessary expenses, optimize your savings, or plan for a major purchase, your AI is ready to help. Simply ask with your specific requirements, and get thoughtful recommendations to ensure every big purchase fits perfectly into your financial plan.


Subscription Tracking


Stay on top of your subscriptions with ease. Your AI keeps track of all your recurring payments, ensuring you never miss a due date. Get timely alerts before any charges hit your account, giving you the chance to review, adjust, or even cancel subscriptions if needed. No more surprise deductions—just seamless subscription management that helps you stay in control of your finances.


Magic Receipt Tracking


Effortlessly manage your spending with automated receipts. Your AI keeps track of payment receipts, logging them instantly for better financial insights. No more manual tracking—every transaction is organized, categorized, and ready for review whenever you need it. Whether it’s a grocery run, a subscription fee, or a big purchase, your AI ensures you have a clear record of where your money goes, helping you stay informed and in control.
